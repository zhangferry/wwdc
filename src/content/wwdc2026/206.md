## 官方章节速览

- **0:00 · Introduction**：An overview of what's new across Apple services, declarative device management, app management, identity integrations, and education technologies.
- **0:41 · Apple services**：Apple Business, a new all-in-one platform now available in over 200 countries, with new APIs for Blueprints, configurations, users, and audit events, plus a volume licensing mechanism for App Store subscriptions.
- **2:34 · Device management**：Declarative management as the new standard, including a managed migration feature that moves data to a new Mac while preserving enrollment and settings.
- **9:33 · App management**：Declarative app configuration comes to macOS 27 with hardware-bound keys and Managed Device Attestation, package file cleanup on removal, and new privacy settings controls.
- **14:56 · Identity Integrations**：Platform SSO enhancements on macOS 27, including a new login and unlock experience and the option for administrators to require Touch ID as a built-in second factor.
- **19:46 · Education**：Authenticated Guest Mode comes to Shared iPad, and a new guided browsing feature in the Classroom app lets teachers lock students to specific websites or a single tab.
