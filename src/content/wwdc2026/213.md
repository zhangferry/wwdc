## 官方章节速览

- **0:00 · Introduction**：Translate your app using agents in Xcode. Xcode considers the context of strings used in your app and includes that in the feed to translation agents.
- **1:49 · Add translations**：Ask an agent to translate your app, then watch as Xcode prepares the project, builds all targets to discover strings, and populates translated String Catalogs.
- **8:11 · Review and iterate**：See how to review the outcome of translations, identify UI truncation issues, and iterate on the localization of your app.
