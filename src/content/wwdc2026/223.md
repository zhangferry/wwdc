## 官方章节速览

- **0:01 · Introduction**：Live Activities keep people up-to-date about ongoing tasks or events with progressing information over time, appearing on the Lock Screen, in the Dynamic Island, on Apple Watch, and on the CarPlay Dashboard.
- **1:53 · Create and update**：Start by defining an efficient data model using ActivityAttributes for static data and ContentState for dynamic data. Construct tailored UI for each presentation and manage the activity's lifecycle locally via ActivityKit or remotely using push notifications.
- **9:51 · Optimize**：Refine the Live Activity experience by adapting layouts to accommodate specific constraints, such as limited width in the Dynamic Island in landscape, or adopting the small activity family for Apple Watch. Integrate App Intents to let people perform quick, contextual actions.
