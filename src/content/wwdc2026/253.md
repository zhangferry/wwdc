## 这场 Session 讲了什么

探索 Music Understanding 这个新框架，让你的 App 能在设备端从六个维度分析音频：调性、节奏、结构、速度、乐器活动和响度。你还将利用“Music Understanding Lab”示例 App 直观地查看各个结果。

## 官方章节速览

- **0:00 · Introduction**：Discover how the Music Understanding framework brings on-device offline audio analysis to all Apple platforms.
- **1:39 · Musical features**：Explore the six areas of the framework's music analysis: key, rhythm, structure, pace, instrument activity, and loudness.
- **3:19 · Framework integration**：Learn how to initialize a MusicUnderstandingSession and begin analysis with an AVAsset or custom audio provider.
- **3:55 · Music Understanding Lab**：Walk through a sample app that visualizes all analysis types from the framework.
