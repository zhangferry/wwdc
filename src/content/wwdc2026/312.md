## 官方章节速览

- **0:00 · Introduction**：Discover the Now Playing system experience, available across all Apple platforms. It allows apps to surface currently playing media info on system surfaces like the Lock Screen, Dynamic Island, and CarPlay.
- **1:08 · Media sessions**：Learn how to use the media sessions API to bring audio or video from your app into the system's Now Playing experience by adopting the MediaSessionRepresentable protocol.
- **5:03 · Remote media sessions**：Discover how to extend playback control to devices like smart speakers by adopting RemoteMediaSessionRepresentable and utilizing Apple Push Notification service (APNs).
- **10:31 · Media sharing extensions**：Find out how Media Sharing Extensions simplify routing media from iPhone to other devices by leveraging the system device picker without needing to embed additional SDKs.
