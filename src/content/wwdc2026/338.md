## 官方章节速览

- **0:00 · Introduction**：Apple Immersive Video live streaming transports fans to sports, music, and entertainment events on Apple Vision Pro — illustrated by courtside LA Lakers games delivered live through the Spectrum SportsNet and NBA apps.
- **2:08 · Live production overview**：A high-level overview of the fundamental components and creative tools that make up a modern live production pipeline, from cameras and graphics to video switchers and audio consoles.
- **5:16 · What makes immersive live different**：Discover the unique scale and fidelity required for delivering Apple Immersive Video, including massive video resolutions, high frame rates, and rich Apple Spatial Audio Format (ASAF) mixes.
- **7:05 · Immersive live format**：Learn about the core formats powering live immersive workflows, including streaming ProRes, uncompressed PCM audio, and per-frame JSON metadata.
- **9:09 · Real-time media transport**：Explore how live immersive feeds are transported between devices in real time over IP using the SMPTE 2110 industry standard.
- **11:25 · Recording and playback**：Learn how live streams are recorded to disk and played back using AVAssetWriter and the Immersive Media Support framework.
